COVID-19 UPDATE

To All CoCo Fans,

CoCo Fresh Tea & Juice Ontario is committed to caring for the health and well-being of our customers and staff.

Starting March 17, only TAKE-OUT and delivery service will be available at all CoCo Fresh Tea & Juice Ontario stores, until further notice. Reduced operating hours will be adopted at some stores depending on the store locations. Follow us on Instagram or Facebook for the most updated status on our stores.

Below is what you can expect:

* We are pausing the use of all in-store seating.

* Customers can still order take-out at the counter or use delivery.

* To avoid physical contact for the safety of our customers and staff, we ask you to please pay with a Credit or Debit card if possible.

* Please kindly respect social distancing when you are lining up.

Please accept our apologies for any inconvenience. We will continue to adjust our practice in support of health officials and government leaders. Please stay safe and healthy!
